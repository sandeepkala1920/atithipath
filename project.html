Cultural Calendar

January (Tentative)

Makar Sankranti

A festival celebrating the harvest and the sun's movement into Capricorn. Marked by traditional songs, dances, and kite flying.

March-April (Tentative)

Sarhul Festival

A major tribal festival celebrating the arrival of spring. The Sal trees are worshiped as a sign of respect for nature.

May-June (Tentative)

Jitia Festival

A tribal festival where mothers fast and pray for the safety and longevity of their children, celebrated with ritual songs and dances.

August-September (Tentative)

Karam Festival

Celebrated to invoke the Karam Devta (god of fate), this festival is marked by singing, dancing, and the planting of a Karam tree branch.

October-November (Tentative)

Sohrai Festival

A harvest festival where tribal people paint their houses with beautiful murals and worship livestock.

December (Tentative)

Karma Puja

A festival to worship the Karma tree, symbolizing youth and vitality, including traditional dances and music around the tree.
